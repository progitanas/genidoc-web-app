FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does online appointment booking work?

Search for your doctor or specialty, check real-time availability, choose a time slot and confirm. You receive an instant confirmation by email and SMS with all the details (address, instructions, documents to bring).

Are teleconsultations as effective as in-person appointments?

For many cases (prescription renewal, follow-up consultation, medical advice), teleconsultation is perfectly suitable. The doctor can assess your symptoms, prescribe medication, and refer you if a physical examination is necessary.

How is my medical data protected?

We apply bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all your data. Strict GDPR compliance, servers hosted in Morocco with ISO 27001 certification. Only you and the professionals you authorize can access your medical record.

Can I use GeniDoc with my health insurance?

Yes! We partner with many Moroccan health insurance providers (CNSS, CNOPS, private mutuals). Reimbursements can be processed directly through the app. Check the list of partner insurance companies in your profile.

What to do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a life-threatening emergency (chest pain, difficulty breathing, hemorrhage), call 141 (SAMU) immediately. GeniDoc offers a non-life-threatening emergency service 24/7 for situations requiring a quick but non-critical consultation.

Can I view my complete medical history?

With a Premium or Family account, your medical history is centralized and accessible 24/7: past consultations, prescriptions, test results, vaccinations, allergies. You can share it with any healthcare professional with one click.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes. All plans include 24/7 email and chatbot support. Pro and Enterprise plans have access to priority support by phone and video chat. Enterprise customers benefit from a dedicated account manager.

What is the free trial period?

Pro and Enterprise customers enjoy a 14-day free trial without a credit card. The Starter plan is free indefinitely with 5 offers/month.
